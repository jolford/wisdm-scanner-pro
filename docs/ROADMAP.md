## Feature Request Process

We actively incorporate customer feedback into our roadmap:

1. **Submit Request**: Contact your Customer Success Manager or email product@wisdm.io
2. **Review**: Product team reviews within 5 business days
3. **Prioritization**: Quarterly roadmap review with customer input
4. **Updates**: Notify requesting customers when feature ships

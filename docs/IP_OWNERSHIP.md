### Data Processing

WISDM processes your data solely for:
1. Providing the contracted service
2. Improving service quality (aggregated, anonymized)

## Acquisition / Exit Rights

### If WISDM is Acquired

Existing contracts remain in effect under:
- Assignment clauses to successor entity
- Continuation of support obligations
- Price protection for contract term
- Data portability guarantees

### If Customer is Acquired

| License Type | Transfer Rights |
|--------------|:---------------:|
| SaaS Subscription | With written consent |
| On-Premises | Per contract terms |
| Enterprise Agreement | Typically transferable |

### Contract Termination

Upon termination:
1. **Data Export**: 90-day window to export all data
2. **Data Deletion**: Certified destruction within 30 days after export window
3. **License Termination**: No continued access to platform
4. **Custom Work**: Per individual contract terms
